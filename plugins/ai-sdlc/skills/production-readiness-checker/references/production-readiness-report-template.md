## Status Indicators

### 🟢 Ready (Green)
- All required checks passed
- All or most recommended checks passed
- Minor or no recommendations
- Low risk deployment

### 🟡 Ready with Concerns (Yellow)
- All required checks passed
- Some recommended checks failed
- Clear mitigation plan exists
- Medium risk, requires close monitoring

### 🔴 Not Ready (Red)
- One or more required checks failed
- Multiple critical issues
- No clear mitigation
- High/critical risk - do not deploy

---

## Scoring Guidelines

**100-90%**: Excellent - Full production ready
**89-75%**: Good - Ready with minor concerns
**74-60%**: Fair - Ready with significant concerns
**59-40%**: Poor - Major improvements needed
**<40%**: Critical - Not ready for production

**Category Scoring**:
- Each check worth points
- Required checks weighted higher
- Deployment blockers = 0% for that category
- Average category scores for overall score
